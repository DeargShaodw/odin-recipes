Creme Brul

A wonderful dessert

Ingredents

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup heavy creame
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Creme Brulee is a custard made by water bath baking



make cusdard over saucepan double boiler if possible put in over

Steps

  1. Oven 300*F
  2. Egg Yolk Sugar an vanilla and whisk till smooth
  3. heat slowly to simmer in sauce pan double boiler if possilbe
  4. prepare water bath and ramikins
  5. fill ramakins with custard
  6. put in water bath at 300 for 30 min or until set
  7. Chill 2 hours
  8. brulee the sugar topping
